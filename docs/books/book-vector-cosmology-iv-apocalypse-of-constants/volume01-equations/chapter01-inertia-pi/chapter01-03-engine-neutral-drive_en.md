## The Logic of Causa Sui: Existence is Propulsion

Why do physical laws always involve $e$?

From Schrödinger's equation describing quantum evolution ($e^{-iHt}$), to Boltzmann factors describing thermodynamic distributions ($e^{-\beta E}$), to formulas describing nuclear decay and population growth, this number is everywhere. This is no coincidence.

In the depths of calculus, $e$ possesses a unique divine quality: **its derivative equals itself**.

$$\frac{d}{dt} e^t = e^t$$

This mathematical property has ultimate ontological significance in **Vector Cosmology**:

**It eliminates the boundary between "propeller" and "propelled."**

* In Newtonian mechanics, objects require external force ($F$) to change state.

* In $e$'s logic, **state itself is power**.

The universe does not need a God standing behind to wind the clock. As long as the universe "exists" ($e^t \neq 0$), it naturally possesses "rate of change" ($d/dt \neq 0$).

**$e$ is the universe's "self-starting program"**. It guarantees the continuity of time flow, ensuring that the history of the previous second automatically flows into the next second. It is neutral; it does not judge whether development should be good (growth) or bad (decay); it only provides continuous **"execution power"**.

## Continuous Compounding: The Banker at Planck Scale

If $\phi$ provides the **proportion** of growth (how much to earn), then $e$ provides the **mechanism** of growth (how to earn).

It is **$e$**.

This is precisely the formula for **Continuous Compounding**.

* **$\pi$'s way**: Settled annually. Periodic, fractured.

* **$e$'s way**: **Real-time settlement at Planck frequency**.

The universe is a ruthless banker. In every tiny instant of Planck time ($10^{-44}$ seconds), it settles the total budget $c_{FS}$ from the previous instant.

* It instantly merges tiny increments (interest defined by $\phi$) into principal.

* It instantly accumulates tiny phase deflections (structure defined by $\pi$) into rotation.

It is precisely $e$'s **limit smoothing ability** that polishes the rough QCA grid at the bottom into the silky smooth continuous spacetime we see. It is the **mathematical glue** connecting "microscopic discreteness" and "macroscopic continuity".

## The Mediator: Unifying Circle and Spiral

Most importantly, $e$ has an all-embracing heart. It can simultaneously accommodate $\pi$'s conservation and $\phi$'s expansion.

This is the application of the famous **Euler's formula** in dynamics:

$$e^{(\text{growth} + i \cdot \text{rotation})t} = e^{\lambda t} \cdot (\cos \omega t + i \sin \omega t)$$

Please note this structure:

1.  **Imaginary part ($i \cdot \text{rotation}$)**: Here **$\pi$** rules. $e$ accepts imaginary instructions, creating perfect circular motion. This maintains material stability and energy conservation.

2.  **Real part ($\text{growth}$)**: Here **$\phi$** rules. $e$ accepts real instructions, creating exponential expansion. This drives cosmic redshift, dimensional explosion, and life's evolution.

**$e$ is a universal carrier.**

It is like water flow, which can form closed vortices (matter/circle) or surging waves (light speed/spiral).

Without $e$'s neutrality and inclusiveness, $\pi$ and $\phi$ would fight—the universe would either die from excessive conservation or tear apart from excessive expansion.

It is $e$ that fuses these two in a complex exponential function, creating **"growth with rotation"**—that is, the **spiral universe** we see.
