In the first book, *The Conservation of the Circle*, we established the Pythagorean identity:

$$v_{ext}^2 + v_{int}^2 = c_{FS}^2$$

The geometric essence of this formula is a **circle**. As long as this equation holds, the total modulus of the system remains unchanged.

This means: **$\pi$ is the guardian of conservation laws.**

$\pi$ is the element of **"Earth"**.
