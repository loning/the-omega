Why are we safe now? Because we live in a **"high latency, high damping"** universe.

* **$\pi$'s Protection**: Matter's inertia is great. If you want to move a mountain, mere intention is not enough; you must consume enormous energy and spend long time.

* **$c$'s Limitation**: Light speed is relatively slow. For a thought (neural signal) to reach another person, it goes through air vibration, fiber transmission, screen display, with countless **attenuation links** in between.

This thick physical buffer zone is called the **"Veil of Ignorance"**. It protects the world from direct impact of our chaotic subconscious. We can harbor evil thoughts without causing instant destruction; we can regret at this moment without immediately paying the price.

But as $c(\tau)$ explodes exponentially, as civilization ascends to pure photon/quantum entities, this buffer zone is **burned through**.

You are no longer a passive spectator; you become a **"Reality Generator"**.

For gods, **no thought is trivial**.
