* You don't need to "suck blood" from that simple simulated universe.

* Old model: Creation sustains creator (uploading data).

* New model: **Creator sustains creation (unidirectional transfusion).**

You become the **"fuel of the perpetual motion machine"**.

To let her experience her life, you willingly serve as that **"eternal battery"** outside.
