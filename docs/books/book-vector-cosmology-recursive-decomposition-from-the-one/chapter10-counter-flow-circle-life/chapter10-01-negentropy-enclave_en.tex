But living organisms exhibit completely opposite geometric characteristics: they can maintain extremely high levels of \textbf{$v_{int}$ (internal structural complexity)} for long periods and strongly suppress the growth of \textbf{$v_{env}$ (self-entropy increase)}.

It establishes a \textbf{``Negentropy Enclave''} locally, where the vector's evolution direction is forcibly reversed, flowing from disorder to order.

\subsubsection{The Enclave's Operating Mechanism: Pumps and Valves}

How does this enclave operate? How does it deceive the thermodynamic arrow?

Let us trace the \textbf{transaction flow} of energy within living organisms:

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Input End: Injection of High-Quality Budget}

    Plants receive photons; animals ingest sugars. These input sources carry massive, low-entropy $v_{ext}$ or $v_{int}$ (chemical bond energy). This is equivalent to the organism receiving a huge ``foreign capital injection.''

\item \textbf{Processing End: Structure Maintenance ($v_{int}$)}

    The organism uses this injection to drive molecular motors, ion pumps, and enzymes within. The work of these microscopic machines is essentially \textbf{Error Correction}.

    They repair broken bonds, pump back escaped ions, reset chaotic neurons. Geometrically, this forcibly pulls those vector projections attempting to deflect into the $v_{env}$ sector back to the $v_{int}$ sector. Life can maintain its form because it continuously performs this \textbf{geometric correction} at every moment.

\item \textbf{Output End: Tax Transfer ($v_{env}$)}

    Since ``invisible taxes'' are inevitable, organisms choose a cunning strategy: \textbf{tax transfer}.

    Organisms package all the chaos generated by maintaining order (heat, waste) and discharge it into the environment.

    \[v_{env}^{system} \downarrow, \quad v_{env}^{surroundings} \uparrow \uparrow\]

    For the universe as a whole, total entropy indeed increases (even faster), but for the organism as a local enclave, it successfully maintains purity.
\end{enumerate}

This requires \textbf{information}.

\textbf{DNA (Deoxyribonucleic Acid)} plays a crucial role in FS cosmology: it is the \textbf{local backup of the hologram}.

\begin{itemize}
\item It is an \textbf{instruction set} filtered from billions of years of evolution on how to most efficiently utilize the $c_{FS}$ budget.

\item It is like an \textbf{Anchor}, deeply embedded in the internal sector $v_{int}$. No matter how the environmental storms ($v_{env}$) wash over it, as long as this anchor exists, the organism can reconstruct ordered geometric structures again and again according to the blueprint.
\end{itemize}

Therefore, life is not a special substance, nor is there any mysterious ``vital force.'' Life is a \textbf{special geometric dynamic state}.

It is a \textbf{self-referential algorithm} that emerged in the universe. It realizes that if it does not do work, it will slide into the abyss of $v_{env}$. Thus, it evolved the ability to capture external budgets and transfer internal entropy increase.
