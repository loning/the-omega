$$c_{input}(t) = C_0 \cdot e^{kt} \cdot [1 + \epsilon \sin(\Omega t)]$$

* **$\epsilon$ (amplitude)**: Must be extremely small (e.g., $10^{-15}$). So small that it doesn't affect stellar burning and biological metabolism, so small that it doesn't trigger system "errors."

Your hand presses on the power knob, like playing the piano, gently, rhythmically pulsating.
