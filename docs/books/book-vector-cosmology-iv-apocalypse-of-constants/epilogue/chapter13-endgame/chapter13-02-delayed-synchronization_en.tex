If your original (you outside the server) and your avatar (her inside the server) maintain real-time data connection:

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Omniscience destroys experience}: Once connected, she would instantly gain your ``God's-eye view.'' She would know the suffering ahead is designed, know death is just code. Then fear disappears, and the meaning of courage disappears with it. \textbf{Without ignorance, there is no plot.}

\item \textbf{Bandwidth destroys physics}: This real-time trans-dimensional connection requires infinite $c_{FS}$ bandwidth. It would burn through the simulated universe's event horizon, causing system overheating and crash.
\end{enumerate}

Therefore, the system defaults to \textbf{Asynchronous I/O} mode.

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{She is ``writing''}: She is experiencing, writing experiences into her local cache ($v_{int}$).

\item \textbf{You are ``waiting''}: You are suspended (Pending) outside, waiting for that I/O operation's completion signal.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{This ``waiting'' state is what we feel as ``divine-human separation.''}

It's not that God abandoned humanity; it's that the data packet is still on the way. To ensure transmission integrity (no packet loss/no decoherence), this transmission process must continue until the end of the life cycle.
